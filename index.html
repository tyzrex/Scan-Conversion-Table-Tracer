DDA Algorithm

Step 1: Take points (x0, y0) and (x1, y1) as input.
Step 2: Calculate dx = x1 - x0 and dy = y1 - y0.
Step 3: Calculate steps = max(abs(dx), abs(dy)).
Step 4: Calculate xinc = dx / steps and yinc dy / steps.
Step 5: Assign x=x0 and y=y0.
Step 6: Calclate points xi = x + xinc and yi = y + yinc for i = 0 to steps.

Breseham Algorithm

Step 1: Take points (x0, y0) and (x1, y1) as input.
Step 2: Calculate dx = x1 - x0 and dy = y1 - y0.
Step 3: Calculate m = dy / dx.
Step 4: Assign x=x0 and y=y0.
Step 5: Check if(m>0 && m<1) then
                Calculate p = 2 * dy - dx.
Step 6: while(x1)
                if(p<0)
                            then p = p + 2 * dy and x = x + 1.
                else p = p + 2 * (dy - dx) and x = x + 1 and y = y + 1.
Step 7: else if(m>1) then
                Calculate p = 2 * dx - dy.
Step 8: while(y1)
                if(p<0)
                            then p = p + 2 * dx and y = y + 1.
                else p = p + 2 * (dx - dy) and x = x + 1 and y = y + 1.
